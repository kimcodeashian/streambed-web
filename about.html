WHO WE ARE + WHAT WE DO

In a world where content is king, we put the power back in the hands of the creators.

Streambed is a publishing suite and analytics engine that uses blockchain technology to link content across the internet. Streambed aggregates different versions of the same video (including their remixes, memes, clips, compilations etc.) and maps them for the creators and brands that created them, quantifying virality and total impact. By sharing stats between co-creators, brands, sponsors, and talent, everyone benefits from transparent video and social analytics.

OUR MISSION

Streambed’s mission is to link the creative world by returning to contributors the information they deserve about their content. We empower creators and reward those who make valuable contributions to online content. We are built on transparency, and take a privacy-first approach to social media. Streambed allows anyone to search, verify, and act on a piece of content’s associated repurposing terms.
