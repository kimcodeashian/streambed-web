WHAT IS STREAMBED MEDIA?

Streambed Media is a mediatech company founded in 2018 by Michael Casey, Jenna Pilgrim, Joseph Fiscella, and Lance Koonce.

Streambed is a publishing tool and analytics engine that helps link creators and contributors online. It uses the blockchain to get analytics directly from the source, and prove the provenance of media.

THE STREAMBED APP

The Streambed app allows you to publish to Youtube and Twitter from a single dashboard, view advanced analytics, and share traffic details with co-stars, collaborators, funders, and brands.

The Streambed interactive dashboard pulls and combines information from your verified collaborators, showing you a full picture of all of the different versions of your videos. Your collaborators share a unified dashboard to see what you see as the creator.

Streambed indexes each piece of content with a public, immutable record of a video’s existence online, like a bulletproof IMDB or Wikipedia. Every time new content is uploaded through streambed, an index page is generated containing all verified proofs for that content.

Once tagged, verified collaborators are shown publicly on the index pages so all can see who contributed to the production of a new piece of content. These pages also link back to the original video, so users can always trace the content’s provenance.
